Class: Ground

Ground

Class representing Ground Sprite.

Constructor

new Ground(config)

Create a Ground Sprite. This sprite is created with Physics enabled (to enable collision detection) but unaffected by gravity and is immovable. Automatically scrolled in negative x-axis (a capability of Phaser.TileSprite object)
